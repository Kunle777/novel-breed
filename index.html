Our Story

Over 20 Years of Agricultural Excellence

Novel Breed Agric Limited was founded with a clear mission: to produce premium quality animal feed that empowers Nigerian farmers and drives food security. Today, operating from our Ibadan factory and guided by a team of ICAN Fellows, Registered Animal Scientists, and industry veterans, we stand as a trusted name in Nigerian agriculture.

With combined leadership experience of over 100 years across finance, operations, nutrition, and sales — we bring institutional-grade expertise to every bag of feed we produce.
