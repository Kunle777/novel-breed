Who We Are

Nigeria's Trusted Animal Feed Manufacturer

Novel Breed Agric Limited is a privately owned Nigerian company incorporated on 4th November 2022 under the Companies and Allied Matters Act (CAMA) 2020, with Company Registration Number 1994829.

Operating from our registered office in Magboro, Ogun State, and our production facility at Km 6, Old Lagos Road, Podo, New Garage, Ibadan, Oyo State — we are committed to producing scientifically formulated, high-quality animal feeds that empower Nigerian farmers and drive national food security.

Our management team brings together over 20 years of combined expertise across agricultural finance, feed production, animal nutrition, and sales — giving Novel Breed the institutional depth to deliver consistently on quality, compliance, and customer service.

Mission & Vision

Empowered Farmers.

What We Stand For

Our Core Values

These principles guide every decision we make — from the factory floor to the boardroom.

01

Integrity

We conduct every aspect of our business with honesty, fairness, and professionalism — holding ourselves accountable to our customers, partners, and stakeholders.

02

03

Our Journey

How We Got Here

Built on decades of agricultural expertise and a clear vision for Nigerian food security.

Foundation

Novel Breed Agric Limited Incorporated

The company was formally incorporated on 4th November 2022 under CAMA 2020, with RC No. 1994829 — bringing together a team of seasoned ICAN Fellows, Registered Animal Scientists, and livestock industry veterans under one roof.

Operations

Factory Established in Ibadan, Oyo State

Production operations commenced at our Ibadan facility — Km 6, Old Lagos Road, Podo, New Garage — equipped for the manufacture of scientifically formulated feeds for poultry, livestock, and aquaculture markets.

Governance

Corporate Governance Framework Published

Novel Breed formalised its Internal and External Corporate Governance Controls (Authorisation Policy), establishing clear board structures, financial authority limits, audit committees, and risk management frameworks — positioning the company for institutional-grade partnerships.

Today

Growing Across Nigeria

With headquarters in Magboro, Ogun State and a production facility in Ibadan, Novel Breed Agric continues to expand its distribution network, deepen farmer relationships, and build a brand synonymous with quality and trust in Nigerian agriculture.
