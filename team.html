Leadership & Expertise

Our management team combines over 20 years of experience each across agricultural finance, feed production, animal nutrition, and livestock sales — bringing institutional depth to every decision we make.

Managing Director

Adewale Adekunle Adeniyi

ICAN Fellow FCA Agricultural Finance

Adewale is a Fellow of the Institute of Chartered Accountants of Nigeria (ICAN) and the driving force behind Novel Breed Agric Limited. With over 20 years of experience in the Agricultural Industry, he has served in a Director-level role at one of Nigeria's leading Agricultural Concern Companies for over 15 years.

Throughout his career, he has held successive leadership positions including Head of Operations, Head of Finance, Head of Procurement, and Head of Commercial Operations — giving him a comprehensive command of the full agricultural business value chain.
